# Project: Cursed Blood (Working In Progress!)

A dark narrative adventure visual novel built with the **Ren'Py** engine. Step into a world of political intrigue, forbidden secrets, and a race against time to rescue someone whose very existence is a threat to the ruling elite.

---

## The Story

In a sprawling metropolis where humans and diverse species coexist, peace is maintained by a powerful, elite ruling Family. They claim their absolute authority is backed by a pure, divine blessing. 

But their empire is built on a monumental lie.

**Avalice** appears to be an ordinary citizen, but she is actually an anomaly—the last living bearer of a forbidden, cursed bloodline believed to have been wiped out centuries ago. Her blood is the ultimate physical proof of the ruling family's dark history. If the public ever discovers her, the elite's reputation will shatter, and their regime will collapse overnight.

To protect their perfect image, the ruling Family launches a silent hunt. Avalice’s cover is blown, and she is captured. 

They don't just want her imprisoned; they need her entirely erased from history.

### Your Mission
Step into the shadows as the Main Character. With the ticking clock against you, you must infiltrate the enemy family's territory, navigate a web of suspicion, and rescue Avalice before her existence—and her life—is permanently erased.

---

## Core Mechanics & Gameplay Concept

While the game is heavily story-driven, it blends traditional visual novel choices with strategic survival elements:

*   **Suspicion System:** Your infiltration choices matter. Making too much noise or failing to maintain your cover raises the rival family's *Suspicion Meter*. If it hits 100%, the stronghold goes on lockdown.
*   **Dynamic Choice Branches:** Decide whether to rush the rescue recklessly (saving time but risking capture) or take a slower, stealthier approach (keeping suspicion low but increasing the pressure on the hostage).
*   **Atmospheric Storytelling:** Features custom cinematic text delivery, slow camera transitions, and immersive audio styling to enhance the dark fantasy tone.
